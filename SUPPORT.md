## Asking a question or reporting a bug

- **Bug or feature request:** open a
  [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/bigg01/claude-ci-agent/issues) — include your
  platform (GitLab/GitHub, OpenShift/AKS), versions, and steps to reproduce.
- **Questions / ideas:** use
  [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/bigg01/claude-ci-agent/discussions) if
  enabled, otherwise open an issue.
- **Contributing a fix:** see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Security issues

**Do not** open a public issue for vulnerabilities. Report them privately per the
[Security Policy](SECURITY.md) (or email the maintainer at <o.guggenbuehl@gmail.com>).
